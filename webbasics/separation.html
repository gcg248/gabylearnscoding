What is Dog Separation Anxiety?

How long can your dog be alone for?





If your dog can be by him or herself (without any other person or animal) for an hour or less without panicking, your dog may suffer from separation anxiety.

Other signs of separation anxiety can show up as the following behaviors when a dog is left alone:

  1. yelping, barking, or howling
  2. trembling
  3. destructive behaviors

However, these symptoms can also be associated with other conditions, so always consult your veterinarian before assuming separation anxiety is the problem.
