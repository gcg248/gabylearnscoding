Goldendoodles are a mix of Golden Retrievers and Poodles. Personalities vary, but they share traits from both breeds. See some of their attributes and challenges below:

Breed Positive Attributes Potential Challenges
Golden Retriever loyal, good with children, affectionate require consistent exercise, shed, prone to cancer
Poodle intelligent, trainable, hypoallergenic sassy, stubborn, require consistent mental stimulation
