What is a Goldendoodle?

Click here to learn about goldendoodles: Dolly.

Goldendoodles are a broad umbrella for dogs who have any breed mixture of golden retrievers and poodles, which means that while their personalities and physical characteristics vary, they tend to share overlapping core traits from Goldens and Poodles. See some of their attributes and issues listed below.

Breed Positive Attributes Potential Challenges
Golden Retriever loyal, good with children, affectionate require consistent exercise, shed, prone to cancer
Poodle intelligent, trainable, hypoallergenic sassy, stubborn, require consistent mental stimulation
